Welcome to the Bison Design blog!

Crafting software is an art. My training in mathematics and decades of experience
developing enterprise software gave me a unique perspective to appreciate its beauty. The search for simplicity influenced my designs and this is the subject of my blog.

I hope the excitement I feel upon finding that simplicity comes across in a way that is both educational and entertaining. If you find these pages interesting please share your insights in the comments.
